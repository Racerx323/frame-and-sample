# Glossary

## Frame

A single video image in a sequence.

## Sample

A single discrete audio measurement.

## Frame Rate

Number of video frames per second (for example 30 or 60 fps).

## Sample Rate

Number of audio samples per second (commonly 48000 Hz in video production).

## Drift

Gradual timing mismatch between audio and video clocks.

## Jitter

Short-term timing variance affecting packet or clock stability.
